Website Not Showing on Google

A business website may look fine but still fail to appear for the right searches. The problem may be indexing, weak content, missing sitemap, poor local signals, no Search Console, technical errors, or unclear service pages.

What Axon checks

  • Whether Google can crawl and index the page.
  • Robots.txt, sitemap, canonical tags and page titles.
  • Search Console, GA4 and conversion tracking.
  • Service pages, local keywords, FAQs and structured data.
  • AI-search readiness for Google AI answers and answer engines.
