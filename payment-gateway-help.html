Payment Gateway Setup Help

When a business owner says “I want to integrate payment gateway”, the real need is usually to let customers pay online through a website, booking form, invoice, shop or portal.

What Axon checks

  • Website platform: WordPress, WooCommerce, Shopify, Wix, custom site or portal.
  • Payment method: Stripe, PayPal, PayNow instructions, cards, bank transfer or invoice links.
  • Customer journey: product, deposit, booking, subscription, invoice or service fee.
  • SSL, confirmation emails, success/failure pages, testing and admin notifications.
