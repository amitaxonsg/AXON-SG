Top questions clients ask

Meaning

What is AEO in simple terms?

AEO means organizing website content so it answers client questions clearly and supports answer engines.

FAQ

Are normal FAQ sections enough?

Not always. The questions must be service-specific, useful and written around real buying concerns.

SEO

Does AEO replace SEO?

No. AEO supports SEO by making content clearer, more helpful and easier to understand.

Pages

Where should AEO appear?

Service pages, website revamp pages, AI solution pages, support pages and contact guidance should include answer-ready content.

Clients

Will this help non-technical clients?

Yes. Clear answers reduce confusion and make it easier for clients to take the next step.

Yes. Axon can audit, rewrite, structure and publish AEO content with your existing branding.
