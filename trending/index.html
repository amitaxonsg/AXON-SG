Search visibility is moving beyond SEO and SEM

SEO

Search Engine Optimization

SEO helps Google understand and rank your website. Axon checks page titles, useful content, internal links, sitemap, mobile layout, speed and indexing.

Solution: start with a website visibility audit before spending more on promotion. Axon can review Search Console, GA4 and service pages, then prepare a practical action plan.

SEM

Search Engine Marketing

SEM brings visitors to your website, but the landing page must clearly explain the offer and make enquiry easy.

Solution: connect campaigns to conversion-focused landing pages, enquiry tracking and follow-up. Axon can assist with landing pages, tracking, forms and campaign readiness.

GEO

Generative Engine Optimization

GEO prepares your website for AI-generated answers and recommendations. Your content must be clear, structured and trustworthy.

Solution: structure service pages around who you help, what you do, outcomes, locations and trust signals. Axon can restructure and publish GEO-ready content.

AEO

Answer Engine Optimization

AEO focuses on useful questions and direct answers. It helps both customers and AI answer engines understand your services without guessing.

Solution: replace generic FAQs with real client questions for each service page. Axon can write answer-ready content in your existing website format.

AI Agent

AI agents for business guidance

An AI agent can guide users, explain services, answer common questions and hand over to your team when human help is needed.

Solution: start with a website AI agent that explains your services in non-technical language and routes enquiries. Axon can help integrate, train and maintain it.
