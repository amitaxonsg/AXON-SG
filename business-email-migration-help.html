If you are searching for Google to 365 email migration, Gmail to Outlook, Outlook to Gmail or Microsoft 365 to Google Workspace, you are usually trying to move company email without losing messages or causing downtime.

What Axon checks

  • Current provider: Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, cPanel, Zoho, IMAP or POP.
  • Number of users, aliases, shared mailboxes, groups, calendars and contacts.
  • DNS records including MX, SPF, DKIM and DMARC.
  • Cutover timing, user devices, Outlook/Gmail setup and post-migration support.
