Website Form Not Sending Email

If your contact form says “sent” but enquiries do not reach your inbox, the issue is often email delivery, not the visible form. This can affect WordPress, Wix, Shopify, custom websites and hosted forms.

Common causes

  • SMTP not configured or wrong sending address.
  • Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 blocking untrusted mail.
  • SPF, DKIM or DMARC records missing or incorrect.
  • Hosting server mail restrictions or spam filtering.
  • Form plugin notification settings using the wrong recipient.
